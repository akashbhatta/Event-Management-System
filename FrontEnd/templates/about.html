About EventPro Nepal

Built to make event discovery, hosting, and participation simple.

Who We Are

EventPro Nepal is an event management platform focused on connecting organizers and attendees in one clean workflow. You can create events, browse upcoming activities, and register in a few clicks.

The system is designed for community events, professional gatherings, and everything in between.

Core Features

  • Create and manage events
  • Explore categorized listings
  • Register with attendee notes
  • Track hosted and joined events
Our Mission

Make event management accessible and efficient for everyone.

Our Vision

Build the most trusted event platform for local and global communities.

Our Community

Empowering hosts and attendees to create meaningful experiences.
